Flair 58 Plus

Versatile manual espresso machine with a heated reservoir, 58mm portafilter, and stylish walnut accents. Perfect for traditional or modern espresso styles.

$640.00

Flair 58 Plus - Usage

Usage Instructions:

  • Step 1: Preheat the Flair 58 Plus group head by activating the heating system.
  • Step 2: Grind fresh coffee beans to a fine espresso consistency.
  • Step 3: Place the 58mm portafilter onto the scale, add 18-20 grams of coffee, and tamp evenly.
  • Step 4: Insert the portafilter into the group head and lock it securely.
  • Step 5: Add hot water (92-96°C) into the reservoir.
  • Step 6: Pull the lever slowly and steadily to extract the espresso shot.
  • Step 7: Release the lever when the desired amount of espresso is extracted (usually 25-35 seconds).
  • Step 8: Remove the portafilter, knock out the used coffee puck, and clean all parts.

Cleaning: Rinse the portafilter, group head, and water reservoir thoroughly after each use to maintain performance.
